Bur-View

A gamified community mobile app connecting Burlington residents with local environmental activities. Users earn points for tree planting, cleanups, and recycling — then redeem them at local businesses.

Mobile UX/UI Gamification Geo-Location Community App

Overview

Bur-View bridges the gap between individual motivation and collective environmental impact. The app gamifies real-world eco-friendly activities across Burlington with a points system, interactive maps, group competitions, and a local rewards marketplace.

Key Features

Solo Activities

Track personal environmental tasks, accumulate points, and set individual goals.

Group Activities

Form or join teams, compete on leaderboards, and collaborate on community projects.

Points & Rewards

Redeem earned points for real discounts at Burlington's local partner businesses.

Location Discovery

Interactive map showing nearby activities, businesses, and other users in real-time.

Activity Tracking

View completed tasks, history, and progress toward personal or group goals.

Watch Companion

Compact wearable view for Apple Watch with quick stats and active group activities.

Technical Highlights

  • Responsive mobile-first design with intuitive navigation
  • Real-time location tracking and mapping integration
  • Point-based gamification system to drive engagement
  • Social features enabling community building and competition
  • Scalable architecture supporting multiple cities and regions
  • Integration with local business partners for rewards program

Future Plans

  • Expansion beyond Burlington to other cities
  • Social sharing for activity achievements
  • Carbon footprint calculator and impact visualization
  • Corporate sponsorship and enterprise partnerships
  • Push notifications for nearby activities and group updates
